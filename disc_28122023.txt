PO planning - 
- Auto Increment Planning number
- cluster store
- consumption date  exp. 1 nov to 1 dec range
- category of product (filter)
- subcategory of product (filter)
- consumable (filter)
- high value items (filter)
- choose vendor (filter)
- planned for period - exp. 1 nov to 1 dec range
- planned quantity - can be blank
- planned numbers items
- planned - yes/no (default yes)
- item name
- default UOM
- current stock
- ideal order quantity - system calculate, (comsumption-current stock)
- current stock at the time of planning
- remark
- request add new qty
- allowed qty (make changes in balance qty after edit)
- price - can be blank and approximate (average from comsumption_date if not then go for last purchase price)
